I'm Siegobert Jansen, a hospitality manager with a passion for sales, sourcing the best deals and negotiating contracts and all things wine&beer. With over 30 years of experience in the industry, I have honed my skills in managing teams, driving sales, and curating the perfect wine selection for #metdeflesoptafel.

My journey in the hospitality industry started as a youngster, but I quickly climbed the ladder to become a entrepreneur. I have worked in various settings and have always strived to provide an exceptional experience for my guests.

As a sales manager, I have a proven track record of exceeding targets and building strong relationships with clients and stakeholders. I believe that understanding their needs and providing personalized solutions is the key to success in sales.

Being a wine&beer enthusiast, I have a deep understanding of the industry and keep up with the latest trends and developments.

I am a natural leader and thrive in fast-paced, dynamic environments. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow both personally and professionally. I am a firm believer in continuous learning and am always open to new ideas and perspectives.

Aside from my professional achievements, I am a team player and enjoy collaborating with cross-functional teams to achieve common goals. I strongly believe that a supportive and positive work environment is crucial for personal and professional growth.
